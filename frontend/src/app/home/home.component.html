My generation doesn’t cook anymore. Things are fast, most of us don’t have the privilege of our own kitchen, and food delivery apps are so convenient that the laborious task of preparing our own pot of pasta seems more unappealing than ever. Why bother to boil water when we can have Yelp's best-rated burrito schlepped to our fifth-floor walk-up in the time that it takes to remember our dad's Comcast.
